About Me

Hi, I'm Nikhil, a Research Scientist at Google DeepMind (GDM)! My interests span a broad range of topics including efficient multimodal learning, active learning, self-supervised representation learning, visual neuroscience/perception, and AI alignment with human behavior. Prior to GDM, I completed my PhD from New York University under the supervision of Eero Simoncelli and was a member of the Flatiron Center for Computational Neuroscience. I also have BS and MS degrees from Stanford University where I studied a wide-range of things including signal processing, machine learning, numerical methods, optimization and computational biology.

In my free-time I'm an avid tennis player, skiier, rock climber, and classical guitarist.
